Privacy Policy for FeelSpend

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

Introduction

FeelSpend ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

By using FeelSpend, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Data Collection

FeelSpend does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data outside of your device.

All information you enter into the app, including:

  • Expense amounts and descriptions
  • Spending categories
  • Mood selections
  • Challenge completion status
  • Your name (if provided during onboarding)

is stored locally on your device only using Android local storage technologies. No data is sent to external servers.

No Third-Party Services

FeelSpend does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage or backup services
  • Any third-party SDKs that collect user data

Permissions

FeelSpend does not request any sensitive permissions such as:

  • Location
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Phone calls

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Android device using:

  • Room Database for expense and activity records
  • SharedPreferences for app settings and preferences

No data leaves your device at any time.

Internet Usage

FeelSpend does not require an internet connection to function. All features work fully offline.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  1. Going to Android Settings → Apps → FeelSpend → Storage → Clear Data
  2. Uninstalling the app

Since there is no cloud backup, deleted data cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

FeelSpend is intended for use by individuals who are 18 years of age or older. The app is not designed for, directed at, or intended to be used by children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
